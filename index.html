Privacy Policy

CoreBMI

Effective: May 20, 2026 Last Updated: May 20, 2026 Developer: Aziz Apps Dev
Summary: CoreBMI collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything stays on your device. No internet connection is used. No ads. No tracking.

📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how CoreBMI ("the App"), developed by Aziz Apps Dev, handles your information. By using this App, you agree to the practices described here.

CoreBMI is a fully offline BMI calculator. It does not connect to the internet, does not use any third-party SDKs that collect data, and does not contain advertisements.

🚫 Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store remotely, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number, device ID)
  • Health or biometric data (height, weight, or BMI values you enter)
  • Location data of any kind
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device information (model, OS version, network carrier)
  • Advertising identifiers

📱 Local Data Storage

Any BMI results you choose to save are stored exclusively in a local database on your own device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App or clear app data
  • Can be deleted at any time from within the App via the History screen

🌐 Internet Access and Permissions

CoreBMI requests zero permissions. The App does not require internet access, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or any other system permission.

No network requests are made at any point during the use of this App.

📢 Advertising

CoreBMI contains no advertisements. The App does not use Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, or any other advertising or monetization SDK.

👶 Children's Privacy

This App does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for users of all ages and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and Google Play's Families Policy.

⚕️ Disclaimer

CoreBMI is intended for general health awareness purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for any medical concerns.

🔒 Security

Since no data is transmitted outside your device, there is no risk of a data breach from our end. Your BMI records remain as secure as your own device.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be published on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
